Alachua County 2018 Florida House District 21 Democratic Precinct Data

This map shows all of the precincts in Alachua County where Jason Haeseler won 50 percent or more of the vote for the Florida House District 21 seat. Democrat Jason Haeseler won 55.14 percent of the vote in Alachua County. This encompasses Alachua, Dixie and Gilchrist counties. Ultimately, Haeseler lost to incumbent Chuck Clemons.
